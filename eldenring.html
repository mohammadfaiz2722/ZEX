Elden Ring is a groundbreaking action RPG developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco. Co-created by Hidetaka Miyazaki and George R. R. Martin, the game offers a vast, dark fantasy open world filled with mystery, danger, and lore. Players explore the Lands Between, battling monstrous foes and uncovering secrets hidden in every corner.

  • Game ID: ER2022
  • Genre: RPG, Fantasy, Open World
  • Multi-tags: Soulslike, Dark, Epic

Elden Ring expands the formula FromSoftware is known for, offering freedom in exploration and a rich narrative experience. It combines punishing combat with seamless exploration across a massive, interconnected world teeming with dungeons, castles, and formidable bosses.


The game supports multiple builds and playstyles—ranging from sword-wielding warriors to powerful magic users. With atmospheric storytelling and breathtaking visuals, Elden Ring redefines the fantasy RPG genre for a new generation.
